How does life look through the 'pleasure, passion and purpose' lens?

I believe that achieving Higher Purpose then Passion then Pleasure is a very hard thing to do as Pleasure is fleeting and there are more stimulai for it, which makes it easier to obtain than the pursuit of Higher Purpose which takes a lot more effort and focus. So I think the hard work to obtain Higher Purpose will be worth it in the long run, but not being distracted by short term pleasure hunting along the way is important.

What are your core values

My core values are attiude, always looking for the postive, forward thinking, not dwelling on the past, patience, staying calm and not letting my emotions run away with me.

What do you stand for?

As I get older and more experienced what I stand for changes, the things that I find important are community, encouragement, striving and not wasting time, its the only thing we can't get more of.
