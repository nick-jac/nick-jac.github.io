What learnings did you take from chapter one?

The first thing I took from chapter one was that my understanding of self awareness, self reagulation and attention was way off, after watching Shereefs video,

How did you find the two minutes of mindfulness?

Difficult, my mind tends to drift, I put the timer on and tried to focus on my breathing, but found my focus drifting probably four or
five times throughout the two minutes. This is something I will be working on, as after the two minutes was up I did feel 'different' more focused.
I am aiming to try two minutes every time I come back from a long break before starting again. I feel that two minutes of mindfulness already helps
to focus my attention on the tasks at hand.
