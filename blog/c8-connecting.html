A situation arose within a company I used to work for where I was producing some work for a set of clients and a contractor was also producing some similar work and part of both of our respective recipients lapped over.


From my point of view I was creating some work that targeted a specific group.


The contractor was creating a one size fits all product that he wanted to incude this group in.


From my point of view I was trying to target the professional dev of this group to enable more sales through their channel


The contractor was trying to include this group to push a generic piece of work to make his own sales figures better


I won! better product! better outcome!
