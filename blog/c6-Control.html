When programming, what are some of your triggers??

Main triggers are blocks when something is not running or a debugger shows where the error is but I can't see it, or when I need to do something but have a block on how how to start it.

What causes a disproportionate emotional response?

Mental blocking of what should be a simple fix for something and when I'm trying to finish something but am running short of time.

How did you find the Siberian North Railroad exercise (Stop, Breathe, Notice, Reflect, Respond)?

I enjoyed reading about this process. What I try and achieve is two min of mindfulness before starting each session, and now I think I can apply these tools for unexpected interuptions during a session. I like the way you can take a small amount of time to control your reaction to something unexpected which enables you to take control of your emotions and therefore your reaction when dealing with it.
