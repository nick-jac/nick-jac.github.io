What is the relationship between mindfulness and self awareness?

The answer for this question is broken down into two quotes which I will pull straight from Search Inside Yourself. Firstly: Self Awareness is a neutral mode that maintains self reflectiveness even in the midst of turbelent emotions. -Daniel Goleman. Secondly: Mindfulness means paying attention in a particular way: on purpose, in the present moment, and non-judgementally. -Jon Kabat-Zinn They seem to essentially be the same thing, the main difference is that mindfulness is initially something that is learnt and Self Awareness happens naturally. However you can train both, so they end being very similar.
